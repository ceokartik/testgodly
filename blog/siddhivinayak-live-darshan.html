Darshan time at Siddhivinayak temple

The temple authorities follow a strict schedule throughout the week. Apart from Darshan time, various religious ceremonies take place in the temple. Here is the following schedule that is followed daily except on Tuesday.

  • The morning Aarti, Kakad Aarti, starts at 5:30 am and ends at 6:00 am.
  • Darshan timing starts at 6:00 am to afternoon 12:15 pm.
  • From 12:15 pm to 12:30 pm, the temple priest offers Naivedyam to Lord Ganesha.
  • The evening prayer begins at 7:30 pm and finishes at 8:00 pm.
  • The final direction slot starts from 8:00 pm to 9:50 pm.
  • The last Aarti, Shejaarti takes place at 9:50 p.m. The temple remains close till the following day.

Tuesday Siddhivinayak Darshan timings

Siddhivinayak darshan timings on Tuesday differ from regular ones.

  • The early morning Darshan starts at 3:15 pm to 4:45 am.
  • Kakad Aarti begins at 5:00 am and goes on till 5:30 am.
  • Darshan time is longer on this day; it is from 5:30 am to 12:15 pm.
  • Nivedyam timing starts from 12:15 pm to 12:30 pm.
  • Darshan time reopens at 12:30 pm and goes on till 8:45 pm.
  • Night prayer starts from 9:30 pm to 10:00 pm.
  • The last Aarti starts at 12:30 am. The temple doors remain closed the following day. Keep in mind that Darshan is available from a distance from 8:45 pm onwards.
